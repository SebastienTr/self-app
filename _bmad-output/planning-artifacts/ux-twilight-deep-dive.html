The Palette

Why Twilight feels magical: The amber accent (#E8A84C) on deep navy backgrounds creates a natural "light source" effect — like a lantern in darkness. Key advantage over Terra: Twilight's blue-based backgrounds provide naturally higher contrast ratios with both warm (amber) and cool (teal) accent colors. The Terra theme's warm brown backgrounds slightly penalized cool-toned elements like success green. Twilight is contrast-neutral — all semantic colors perform equally well.
The Ambient Magic
Twilight enables three levels of ambient effects that feel natural on the blue-night canvas.
Idle — breathing blue
Resting State
Subtle navy glow, barely perceptible. The app feels alive, like stars behind clouds. 6s breathing cycle.
Agent thinking...
Creation Glow
Warm amber radial glow appears when the agent is working. Faster 3s cycle. The "lantern" turns on — something is being made.
✓ All modules fresh
Settled State
Barely-there multi-point glow. The dashboard is alive but calm. Heartbeat has pre-computed everything. Static, no animation.
No animation
Reduced Motion

Why Twilight as default (not Terra): The navy-blue background is universally neutral — it doesn't impose a "style" the way terra cotta does. Terra says "craft workshop." Twilight says "night sky" — a canvas where anything can appear. This aligns perfectly with Self's promise: one million users, one million apps. The amber accent adds warmth without theming the whole experience.
